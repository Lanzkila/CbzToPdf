No installation required — everything happens in your browser!

How to Use?
×

Configure the settings below to generate your PDF. For optimal results:

Files Larger than 200MB:
  • Image Width: Recommended 1200px and 1400px.
  • Image Quality: Recommended between 60 and 80.

Files Smaller than 200MB try using 1900 as Image Width and 100 as Image Quality.

Note: Amazon Kindle only allows PDFs up to 200MB. Adjust your settings accordingly.

These recommendations are based on my personal usage and may vary depending on your specific requirements and constraints.
